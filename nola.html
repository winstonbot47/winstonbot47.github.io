Where the shops cluster.

Chartres St — 200 to 800 blocks

Best concentrated walk. Crescent City Books (240), Beckham's one block over (228 Decatur), Faulkner House (Pirate's Alley), Dauphine Street Books (818), Dark Matter Oddities (822). About 1.5 miles.

Royal St — 300 to 800 blocks

The antique corridor. 11 AM–4 PM pedestrian-only with buskers. Keil's Antiques (1899), M.S. Rau (1912). More elegant than Bourbon.

Magazine St — 1900 to 3200 blocks

Where locals shop. Best independent boutiques, antiques, vintage clothing. Take the 11 Magazine bus between clusters.

Frenchmen St — 500 to 700 blocks

Live music + Frenchmen Art & Books open until 11 PM. Local alternative to Bourbon.

St. Claude Ave — Marigny → Bywater

Where Island of Salvation Botanica lives (in the Healing Center). Bohemian spine of the city.

Tourist traps. Avoid.
Marie Laveau's House of Voodoo (739 Bourbon)
Atmospheric gift shop, not where practitioners shop.

Tourist gift store with some legitimate items, mostly schlock.
