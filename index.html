About Winston

Winston is a personal AI assistant that sends SMS messages on behalf of Kevin Gardner to a small group of personally known contacts who have opted in. Messages are transactional and informational — never marketing.

What Winston Does

  • Reminders — Meeting times, appointments, follow-ups
  • Scheduling — Coordination messages for events and meetings
  • Informational Updates — Sharing links, contact information, and requested details
  • Conversational Replies — Responding to inbound messages from opted-in contacts

Messaging Details

  • Volume: Low — typically fewer than 50 messages per month total
  • Frequency: Varies; does not exceed a few messages per week per contact
  • Recipients: Account holder and personally known contacts only
  • Content: Non-marketing, transactional/informational only
  • Message and data rates may apply

Consent & Opt-In

All message recipients have provided consent through one of the following methods:

  • Inbound text: The recipient sends a text message to Winston's number, opting in to receive replies
  • Verbal consent: Kevin Gardner obtains verbal confirmation before adding a contact

Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

No mobile information or messaging consent data is shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.
